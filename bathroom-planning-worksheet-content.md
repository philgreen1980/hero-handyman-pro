## Pro Tips from Hero Handyman Pro

**Budget Wisely:** Allocate 10-15% of your budget for unexpected issues like hidden water damage or outdated plumbing that needs upgrading.

**Prioritize Function:** Choose durable, water-resistant materials over trendy finishes that may not hold up in a high-moisture environment.

**Plan for Storage:** Bathrooms never have enough storage. Consider recessed niches, medicine cabinets, and vanities with drawers.

**Don't Skimp on Ventilation:** A quality exhaust fan prevents mold, mildew, and moisture damage that can cost thousands to repair.

**Think Long-Term:** If you plan to age in place, incorporate accessibility features now—they're easier and cheaper to install during a remodel.
